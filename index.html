About Us

The Master Plumbers' Association of Queensland (MPAQ) is the peak industry body representing plumbing contractors throughout Queensland.

Who We Serve.

Our members are individuals or companies engaged in the installation of Gas, Water Reticulation and Irrigation Systems, Fire Protection Services, Heating and Cooling, Mechanical Services/Air Conditioning Systems, Sanitary Disposal, Drainage, Metal Roofing, Wall Cladding and other Plumbing Services.

By offering our services our objective is to protect Community Health and the Environment through Professional Plumbing Services.

How We Serve.

Advancing the plumbing services industry through policy development, representation and the provision of specialist training, advice, products and services.

Our Purpose.

To drive professionalism in the plumbing and gas industry to our members' advantage.

Our Vision.

The peak industry body driving strong and sustainable members by providing quality services and support.

Course Overview

The COVID-19 Awareness for the Plumbing Industry course has been designed for Plumbers and Gasfitters to further their knowledge about the safety requirements needed on a day to day basis within their role. It will provide learners with a broad knowledge of the physical requirements and provide practical advice for the control of the COVID-19 Virus.

A Webcam is required in order to participate in the course and action its content.

Prerequisites

There are no prerequisites for this course, however all learners must possess a valid USI - Unique Student Identifier.

For more information about the USI, and to create your USI please click HERE or contact MPAQ on 0732730800.

Course Delivery

This course is delivered online and is self-paced. The course will take approximately 30 minutes to complete.
